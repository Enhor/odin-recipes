Manhattan

What is better than a fancy cocktail at the end of a long day ?

Nothing

Here is how to achieve that greatness.

You will need ;

  • 2 ounces whiskey
  • 1 ounce sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 lemon twist

Steps ;

  1. Add the bourbon, sweet vermouth and bitter to a mixing glass with ice, and stir until well-chilled
  2. Strain into a chilled coupe
  3. Garnish with a lemon twist
  4. Enjoy the simple things in life
